Advanced Manufacturing Projects

先進製造專案

Through digital technology, I transform ideas and materials into unique design expressions, achieving geometric shapes and innovations that traditional craftsmanship could not reach while embracing the core spirit of craftsmanship.

透過數位科技，我將想法與材料轉化為獨特的設計表達，實現傳統工藝無法達到的幾何形狀與創新，同時擁抱工藝的核心精神。

Skylight House

院之家

Skylight House integrates innovative 3D concrete printing, traditional wooden structures, and metal cladding, using contemporary technology to express the deep cultural heritage of traditional architecture.

院之家的設計概念源自創新的水泥３Ｄ列印工法、傳統木構造和金屬包板的交融，以當代科技呈現傳統建築中的深厚文化底蘊。

Precise Imprecision

精確的不精確

Precise Imprecision is an architectural experiment that blends technological and material innovation, highlighting the balance between robotic precision and the unpredictability of natural forms through flexible formwork.

精確的不精確是一個結合科技與材料創新的建築實驗，通過機械手臂和彈性模板技術，展現出製作的精確性與自然形態的不確定性。

Weaving Stone

岩織

Weaving Stone is a series that combines traditional Japanese weaving techniques with advanced 3D concrete printing technology.

岩織是一個結合日本傳統織物工藝與先進３Ｄ水泥列印技術的系列。

Cycle-Craft

循環築

Cycle-craft draws inspiration from the widespread issue of plastic waste in modern society, prompting a rethinking of people's mistrust of recycled and ocean plastic materials.

循環築的靈感來自於當今社會對塑膠資源的浪費現象，這使我們開始反思人們對回收材料與海洋塑料的不信任。

Space Weaving

空間築

Space Weaving employs advanced robotic 3D printing technology to create intricate three-dimensional grid structures, exploring geometric aesthetics that traditional craftsmanship cannot achieve.

空間築引用了機械手臂的高端３Ｄ列印技術，創造出精細的立體網格結構，探索了傳統工藝無法實現的幾何美感。

Time Imprint

時紋

Time Imprint captures the fleeting ripples formed by raindrops, translating them onto wood. The concentric ripple patterns on the surface symbolize the eternity of time and space.

時紋捕捉了下雨時的水波紋瞬間，轉譯到木頭之上。作品表面的環狀水波紋象徵了時間與空間的永恆。
