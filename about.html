About Ester

關於羅洪芬

Ester Lo (Hong-Fen Lo) is a designer and engineer who seamlessly integrates design, art, and technology. With a master's degree in digital manufacturing from the University of Michigan, she has acquired extensive industry experience and academic collaborations at U.S.-based digital manufacturing companies, Quarra Stone and Eventscape. Ester specializes in leveraging cutting-edge digital technologies and innovative materials while preserving the essence of traditional craftsmanship to create groundbreaking design possibilities. Her work explores the powerful fusion of technology and design, continuously driving her creative journey. Currently, Ester is focused on product development, research, and offering personalized design solutions to clients seeking refined, unique designs for their living spaces.

羅洪芬 (Ester Lo) 是一位專注於將設計藝術與科技結合的設計師與工程師。她擁有密西根大學數位製造碩士學位，並在美國數位製造公司Quarra Stone和Eventscape累積了豐富的產業經驗及產學合作。羅洪芬專精於使用數位科技與創新材料，結合傳統工藝的核心精神，實現前所未見的設計可能性。她在過去的工作中深刻體會到科技與設計的融合如何創造出更多的設計潛力，這也成為她不斷探索的方向。她目前致力於個人產品的研發，並為追求精緻生活與獨特設計的客戶提供個性化設計及解決方案。
