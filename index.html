About Me

關於我

I'm a Creative Technologist specializing in AI automation and advanced manufacturing. With experience across intelligent workflows, digital fabrication, and computational design, I create solutions that are both functional and future-ready.

這幾年，我一直在探索 AI 自動化與先進製造交會的領域。一路上參與過智慧化流程、數位製造與運算設計，也逐漸體會到創意與工程如何交織在一起。

My goal is to help businesses and factories work smarter by integrating AI-driven systems with practical engineering. I believe technology should not only enhance efficiency but also solve real-world challenges in meaningful ways.

我最在意的，是幫助企業與工廠更聰明地運作。讓人們少花時間在重複性工作上，把更多心力放在真正有價值的地方。對我來說，科技不只是追求效率，而是打造能夠真實改變我們生活與工作的工具。
