Cycle-Craft

循環築

Cycle-craft draws inspiration from the widespread issue of plastic waste in modern society, prompting a rethinking of people's mistrust of recycled and ocean plastic materials. Based on this reflection, I carefully selected tested and certified eco-friendly materials and incorporated them into an innovative design language, resulting in this series of works.

Through precise 3D printing technology, Cycle-craft successfully challenges the boundaries of geometry, color, and texture, making it not just an exploration of material usage but a redefinition of design limits. This series creates a proper design and manufacturing cycle ecosystem by combining technology with innovative materials.

Design and Fabrication: Ester Hong-Fen Lo, Ali Seyedahmadian, AiBuild, Eventscape
Sponsor: Eventscape
Location: New York
Year: 2023

循環築的靈感來自於當今社會對塑膠資源的浪費現象，這使我們開始反思人們對回收材料與海洋塑料的不信任。在這一思考的基礎上，我精選了經過測試和認證的環保材料，並將其融入創新的設計語彙中，進而創作出這一系列作品。

通過精密的３Ｄ列印技術，循環築成功地挑戰了幾何形狀、顏色和質感的多層次變化，這不僅僅是材料應用上的探索，更是設計邊界的重新定義。透過科技與創新材料的結合，構建出了一個真正意義上的設計與製造循環生態。

設計與數位製造： 羅洪芬、Ali Seyedahmadian、AiBuild、Eventscape
贊助商： Eventscape
地點： 美國 紐約
年份： 2023
