Space Weaving

空間築

Space Weaving employs advanced robotic 3D printing technology to create intricate three-dimensional grid structures, exploring geometric aesthetics that traditional craftsmanship cannot achieve. With precise control over the material's temperature and the machine's movements, three-dimensional carbon fiber is printed directly into the air. A robust structural grid is woven through variations in printing angles, speed, precision, and temperature, which engages in a dynamic spatial dialogue, enhanced by the interplay of light and shadow.

Design and Fabrication: Ester Hong-Fen Lo, Leon Yi-Liang Ko
Location: University of Michigan, Ann Arbor
Advisor: Wes McGee
Year: 2018

空間築引用了機械手臂的高端３Ｄ列印技術，創造出精細的立體網格結構，探索了傳統工藝無法實現的幾何美感。基於對材料的溫度及機器的精確掌控，三維立體的碳纖維直接被列印在空氣中，通過列印角度、速度、誤差和溫度的變化，編織出一個結構性強、具有光影變化的網格體，進行了流動性的空間對話。

設計與製作： 羅洪芬、柯宜良
地點： 密西根大學安娜堡分校
顧問： Wes McGee
年份： 2018
