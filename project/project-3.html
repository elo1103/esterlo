Weaving Stone

岩織

Weaving Stone is a series that combines traditional Japanese weaving techniques with advanced 3D concrete printing technology. Using precise control by robotic arms, each layer of concrete is extruded in a soft, cake-like state and gradually hardens over time, forming a solid structure with rich textural beauty. These unique woven textures are naturally created by variations in the machine's path design and speed, resulting in cascading effects in the concrete. This gives the impression that the material is being woven into space, creating a visual contrast where the rigid material appears soft and fluid.

Design and Fabrication: Ester Hong-Fen Lo, Ali Seyedahmadian, Sika, Eventscape
Sponsor: Sika, Eventscape
Location: New York
Year: 2023

岩織是一個結合日本傳統織物工藝與先進３Ｄ水泥列印技術的系列。通過精密的機械手臂控制，每一層水泥以柔軟如蛋糕般的狀態被擠出，隨著時間的推移逐漸固化，形成穩固而充滿紋理美感的結構。這些獨特的「織物感」紋理，正是藉由機器的路徑設計與速度變化，自然產生的水泥垂墜效果，彷彿在空間中「編織」出具體的形態，塑造了堅硬材料呈現柔軟感的視覺衝擊。

設計與數位製造: 羅洪芬, Ali Seyedahmadian, Sika, Eventscape
贊助商: Sika, Eventscape
地點: 美國 紐約
年份: 2023
